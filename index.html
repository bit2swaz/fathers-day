The Unspoken Truths

I don’t say this often — maybe I never have — but thank you. For showing up. For doing what needed to be done. Even when it was hard. Growing up, I never understood why you were so strict, or so quiet. But now, slowly, I get it. You were carrying more than I ever realized. And even then, you still gave me everything I needed, even when it meant holding back what you needed.

I know I don’t have all the answers yet. I’m still learning. I'm still figuring things out. But one thing I do know — I will make use of everything you have given me. Whether it’s time, trust, or sacrifice — I will carry it forward with purpose. I don’t know what success looks like yet. But when I get there, whatever “there” looks like — I hope you see it and feel like it was worth it.
